Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 17, 2026

Welcome to BookNest. I am committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your reading experience is secure and entirely within your control.

1. Information I Collect

BookNest does not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal information on my servers.

My app is designed to be a local-first EPUB reader. This means:

  • All the books you import are stored locally on your device.
  • Your reading progress, highlights, notes, and collections are saved locally on your device.
  • I do not require you to create an account or log in to use the app.

2. Permissions

To provide core functionality, BookNest may request the following permissions on your device:

  • Storage Access: Required to scan for and import EPUB files from your device, and to save your reading data and library configurations.

3. Third-Party Services

I do not integrate third-party trackers, analytics, or advertising SDKs in the core reading experience. Your reading habits are your own business.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

I may update my Privacy Policy from time to time. Since I do not collect your contact information, I recommend checking this page periodically for any changes. Changes are effective immediately after they are posted.

5. Contact Me

If you have any questions or suggestions about my Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact me at sharathbasic@gmail.com.
